Commission process

1. Contact: Send an email providing a brief description of your needs or any questions regarding the info process. Essential information to include:

  • Type: Specify the desired artwork type, such as portrait, landscape, or other specific requirements.

  • Timeline: Indicate your preferred timeframe for receiving the artwork.

  • Rights/Usage: Clarify whether the artwork is intended for personal or commercial use.

Providing additional information like sketches, reference images, or other materials can help better understand your vision.

2. Work: Throughout the process, you will receive work-in-progress sketches/drawings to ensure that the artwork aligns with your requirements.

3. Finalization: Once the artwork is completed and approved, the finalization process includes payment.
