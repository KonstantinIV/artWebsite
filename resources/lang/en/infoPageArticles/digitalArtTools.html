Digital Art Tools

With the advancement of AI (Artificial Intelligence) tools, it is important to note that all my artworks are personally created by me using traditional artistic techniques.

Potential consequences of using AI generated content :

  • Brand damage: Many people oppose content created by AI tools due to concerns about authenticity and ethics regarding the creation of AI tools, which can lead to reputation damage for brands.

  • Legal issues: AI tools are often built on copyrighted material without proper compensation or authorization, leading to potential legal disputes and issues when utilizing AI images in projects.

  • Copyright: AI-created images lack originality and may raise issues surrounding copyright ownership and protection.

  • Authenticity:  If half or some of the content is made using AI then it can undermine all the other hard work that was done using the traditional ways.

While AI has positive applications, it is essential to use it responsibly as an assisting tool rather than relying solely on AI-generated content. Relying on AI tools can hinder intellectual growth and create difficulties when facing new challenges without the aid of AI assistance. 

Adding also that I am not entirely anti AI. There are also great AI tools that can help in optimizing workflows but some can be extremly damaging the way they are being used unfortunately.
