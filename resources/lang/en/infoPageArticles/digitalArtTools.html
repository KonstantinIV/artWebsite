Digital Art Tools

With the advancement of AI (Artificial Intelligence) tools, it is important to note that all my artworks are personally created by me using traditional artistic techniques.

Consequences of using AI generated content :

  • Brand damage: Many people oppose content created by AI tools due to concerns about authenticity and ethics regarding the creation of AI tools, which can lead to reputation damage for brands.

  • Legal issues: AI tools are often built on copyrighted material without proper compensation or authorization, leading to potential legal disputes when utilizing AI images in projects.

  • Copyright: AI-created images lack originality and may raise issues surrounding copyright ownership and protection.

Why I choose not to use AI:

  • Bad ethics: AI tools often exploit copyrighted works without permission, which goes against ethical principles. Using AI models trained on such data is unjust to the artists whose work is included.

  • Personal growth: Relying on AI tools can hinder intellectual growth and create difficulties when facing new challenges without the aid of AI assistance.

  • Lack of authenticity: I value the personal touch and uniqueness that comes from creating artwork by hand, expressing my own vision, emotions, and experiences.

While AI has positive applications, it is essential to use it responsibly as an assisting tool rather than relying solely on AI-generated content.
