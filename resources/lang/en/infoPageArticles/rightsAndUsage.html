Rights And Usage

Personal use examples:

  • Printing single T-shirt, poster etc. for personal use.

  • Posting online, using it as a profile picture, etc.

Commercial use examples:

  • Selling prints, T-shirts etc.

  • Advertisement etc.

AI and NFT: Using the received artwork for NFT and AI-related projects is NOT ALLOWED unless agreed upon. If unsure of usages send email regarding your concerns.
