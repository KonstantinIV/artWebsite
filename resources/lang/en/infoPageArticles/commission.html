Commission

Currently I am focusing on improving and learning my craft so I don't have complete commission information for now but if you do still want to contact me regarding commission then here is some initial information that might help. Will be updated in the future.

Contact: Send an email providing a brief description of your needs or any questions regarding the process. Essential information to include:

  • Type: Specify the desired artwork type, such as portrait, landscape, or other specific requirements.

  • Timeline: Indicate your preferred timeframe for receiving the artwork.

  • Rights/Usage: Clarify whether the artwork is intended for personal or commercial use.

Providing additional information like sketches, reference images, or other materials can help better understand your vision.

Work: Throughout the process, you will receive work-in-progress sketches/drawings to ensure that the artwork aligns with your requirements if needed.
